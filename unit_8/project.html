Nicol Bowles is a Mexican American multi-disciplinary artist from the southern tip of Texas.
She began working with different mediums such as graphite,
acrylic, oil, ink, digital design, and photography at a young age before studying Studio Art at the
University of Texas Pan American.


Nicol has instructed childrens art classes and hosted creative events for Valley Artistic Outreach for 4
years before moving to Austin, Texas to pursue a career in web design and digital marketing.
She now spends most of her time traveling, photographing, and working on building her skills in the world
of technology.
